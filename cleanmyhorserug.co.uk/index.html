Clean My Horse Rug

About Us

We are a family owned dedicated horse rug washing service based in Lancashire. We are within easy access of all motorways so we can offer our services to farms and yards all over Lancashire. Our services include reproofing using Nikwax solutions and all items are washed in an antibacterial wash. We also offer a repair service.

As a horse owner I understand the needs required for flexibility around when rugs can be collected or dropped off which is why we offer the option that suits you. When your items are washed, bagged and ready we will text or call you to arrange a collection or delivery to suit you.

Solutions Used

All indoor rugs are washed using Cleanround which is a special antibacterial rug wash that kills 99.9% of bacteria, viruses and fungi. This specially formulated detergent has been scientifically tested and proven to kill ringworm and the bacterium responsible for Strangles and other viruses such as MRSA, Ecoli, Salmonella and C.Diff spores. Cleanround and Nikwax products are environmentally friendly as well as kind to the animals and not tested on animals.

We also offer a repair service, repairs are discussed with the customer at the time of collection. Other small animal items can be washed too such as dog beds and dog coats.
