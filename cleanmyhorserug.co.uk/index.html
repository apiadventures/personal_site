Clean My Horse Rug

About Us

We provide a dedicated horse rug washing service including reproofing outdoor rugs using Nikwax solutions. Let us take the pain away from you having to handle your smelly horse rugs in your own washer by placing an order. We will collect and handle the smelly rug for you, wash and bag the item and send a text or call when your rug is ready for collection or delivery. All items are washed, air-dried, reproofed (if required), simples!

We also offer a repair service, repairs are discussed with the customer at the time of collection. Other small animal items can be washed too such as dog beds and dog coats. Rugs must be paid for upon collection otherwise the rug will be kept until payment is made. We accept cash, cheques or you can pay online via paypal.
