Clean My Horse Rug

About Us

We provide a dedicated horse rug washing service including reproofing outdoor rugs using Nikwax solutions.All indoor rugs are washed with a special antibacterial rug wash that kills 99.9% of bacteria, viruses and fungi called Cleanround. This specially formulated detergent has been scientifically tested and proven to kill ringworm and the bacterium responsible for Strangles and other viruses such as MRSA, Ecoli, Salmonella and C.Diff spores. Cleanround and Nikwax products are environmentally friendly as well as kind to the animals and not tested on animals.

Our rug washing service offers a collect or drop off model to suit your needs, the rug is then washed and bagged as per your order. When your items are ready we will text or call you to arrange a collection or delivery to suit you

We also offer a repair service, repairs are discussed with the customer at the time of collection. Other small animal items can be washed too such as dog beds and dog coats. Rugs must be paid for upon collection otherwise the rug will be kept until payment is made. We accept cash, cheques or you can pay online via paypal.
